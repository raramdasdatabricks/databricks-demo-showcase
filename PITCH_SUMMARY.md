## Slide: "What you'll see" (3 pillars)

**AI agents & apps — from prototype to production**
Build tool-using agents on your own governed data, orchestrate specialists under a supervisor,
and put governed AI directly in users' hands as apps and natural-language analytics.

**Quality & observability — trust you can measure**
Evaluate agents on correctness, safety, and groundedness before launch; trace every request
and monitor quality, cost, and drift in production.

**Governance & compliance — the foundation, not an afterthought**
One permission model for data, models, and agents; automatic end-to-end lineage; and
policy-driven classification and protection of sensitive data.

## Slide: "Why one platform"

- **Speed:** collapse the gap from notebook prototype to governed production service.
- **Trust:** measurable quality and safety, not "it seems to work."
- **Governance by default:** consistent, auditable access across the whole data + AI estate.
- **Less sprawl:** data, analytics, and AI teams on one governed platform — fewer copies, fewer handoffs.
